PANDA

Giant pandas are bears that are native to China, where they are considered a national treasure.

  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • AVERAGE LENGTH:4-5 feet
  • AVERAGE LIFESPAN: 20 years
  • HABITAT: central china

Giant pandas are bear-like in shape with striking black and white markings. The "Giant" Pandas are hardly "Giants". It would take about forty (40) giant pandas to weigh as much as one elephant.
